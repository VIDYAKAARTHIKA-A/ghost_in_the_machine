CORE TOPICS
======================================================================

These topics are extracted from novels by Charles Dickens and Jane Austen.
They represent the major thematic elements found in 19th-century literature.

1. Social Class and Economic Inequality in Victorian Society
2. Marriage, Courtship, and Family Obligations
3. Moral Character and Personal Virtue
4. The Pursuit of Happiness and Contentment
5. Urban Life and Social Transformation
6. Reputation, Honor, and Public Opinion
7. Education, Self-Improvement, and Personal Growth
8. Friendship, Loyalty, and Human Relationships
